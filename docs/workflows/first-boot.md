## Zen Browser notes

- Zen Internet and AW extensions do not expose enterprise policy settings.
- Some policies may require one-time toggles inside the browser UI.

## General checks

- Confirm monitors are detected correctly
- Confirm Waybar launches
- Confirm audio devices appear
- Confirm Tailscale connectivity
- Confirm secrets decrypt correctly
- Confirm browser policies apply
